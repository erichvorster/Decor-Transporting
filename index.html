The Decor story

Décor Transport aims to be the complete transport solution.

Décor Transport was established 1990 in Malelane Mpumalanga. We originally started off as a furniture transporting company. Over time, Décor became a very diverse transport supplier, working in various sectors.

Décor runs a fleet of 34-ton super link trucks. We can deliver big quantities in the Gauteng and Mpumalanga area. Further, we service cross border to Mozambique and Swaziland. We have three depots from where we operate: Malelane, Nelspruit and Wadeville JHB. The depots’ main aim is to support our industrial courier service.

Décor combines part loads from Gauteng to the Lowveld. Further, we supply the same service from Durban KZN. We deliver on a 24 hr basis, using our resources to be very competitive. We strive to fill the gap in the supply chain between every day small parcels and full 34-ton loads. Making it hassle free, and cost-effective.
